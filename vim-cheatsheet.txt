Vim cheatsheet

* Navigation
A to move to the end of the line and switch to editing
I to move to the beginning of the line and switch to editing

* Grepper
grep -irn 'string yang dicari' **/*.php
:lgrep 'quiz_result' **/*.php

* find file by name
find .  -name '*User.php'

* List of Buffers
Buka buffer sebelumnya
:e#
buka buffer. (n = 1,2,3,..)
:e#n  / :buff 4
remove n buffer
:nbw / :nbw!:q
New buffer
:new will create a split window with an unnamed buffer.
:enew will open one in the current window.

Suspend Process

Minimize
ctrl + z
List suspended jobs
jobs 
Open specific jobs
fg %x
Kill specific jobs
kill %x
